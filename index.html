Our Wedding Packages

Destination Wedding Packages

If you’ve always dreamed of tying the knot in a beautiful and exotic location, our destination wedding packages are just what you need. Whether it’s on a sun-kissed beach, in a romantic European city, or amidst the rolling hills of a vineyard, our platform connects you with top-rated wedding venues around the globe. These packages include everything you need, from travel arrangements to venue booking, so you can focus on enjoying your special day.

All-Inclusive Wedding Packages

For those who prefer convenience, our all-inclusive wedding packages are the ultimate solution. These packages bundle everything you need for a perfect wedding into one price. From the venue to catering, flowers, entertainment, and photography, we handle it all. With our all-inclusive wedding packages, you can save time, reduce stress, and have peace of mind knowing every detail is taken care of.

Micro Wedding Packages

For couples who prefer an intimate, low-key affair, our micro wedding packages are ideal.A micro wedding allows you to celebrate with your closest family and friends, focusing on quality and meaningful experiences rather than extravagant details. This package typically includes a smaller guest list, a more intimate venue, and a focus on creating personalized memories.

Customized Wedding Packages

At WedyApp, we believe in making your wedding dreams come true. If none of our pre packaged options fit your vision, we offer customized wedding packages that you can tailor to your exact specifications. From selecting the perfect venue to designing your decor and menu, our professional planners are here to help you create a wedding that reflects your unique style.

Marriage Photography Packages

A wedding is full of moments worth remembering, and our marriage photography packages ensure that every magical moment is captured. Our experienced photographers specialize in both traditional posed shots and candid, natural moments, helping you preserve your memories in stunning images. Whether you're looking for a traditional wedding album or a more contemporary style of photography, we have you covered.
